About Me

Hello! I'm Onesmus Dzidzai Maenzanise, a Fullstack Software Engineer residing in Wynberg, Western Cape, South Africa. I bring a wealth of experience in crafting innovative solutions across a diverse technology stack, with a particular focus on React Native, TypeScript, Python, Java, and SQL.
